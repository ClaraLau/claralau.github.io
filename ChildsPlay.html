Project Overview

Game Git Repository

Dates: September 2014

Description:
Child's Play was an RPG created at the Fall 2014 GI Jam with two themes: Unorthodozed Main Character & Symmetry. The concept behind the game is a scrolling RPG where kids are playing in their backyard. With their imagination however, the backyard becomes a forest and sticks can become swords.

Team Members:
"7 Deadly Sins" 7 University of Waterloo students with 3 programmers, 2 designers, 1 composer and 1 person for moral support!

My Contributions:
I participated in the ideation of the game and created various graphical assets such as character sprites.

Tools Used:
  • Unity
  • Adobe Photoshop
  • PaintTool SAI
  • Dropbox
