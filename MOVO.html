Project Overview

Dates: May- August 2019

Description:
MOVO (short for Movement Violin) is an accessible and expressive digital instrument that is MIDI-compliant and designed to be played by users with upper arm amputations.

Team Members: 5 Systems Design Engineering students with various expertise

Design Process:
The idea for MOVO was created following a 5-day sprint method. Based on the initial idea, an iterative design process was then followed to build a medium fidelity prototype with working software and hardware components that could be presented at a demo concert. Feedback sessions were run with students and people with disabilities passionate about music recruited from the local community.

Methods Used
